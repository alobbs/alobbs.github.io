## 🌍 Climate & Environment

### El Niño is coming. The last time ocean temp. looked like this, millions died

Climate models from NOAA and ECMWF indicate a near-certain probability of a "Super El Niño" forming by this winter, with ocean temperatures potentially reaching levels seen during the catastrophic 1877 event. This phenomenon threatens to cause significant global economic losses, food insecurity, and humanitarian crises worldwide.
