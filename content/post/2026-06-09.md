### Can LLMs Beat Classical Hyperparameter Optimization Algorithms?

A study comparing Large Language Models (LLMs) to classical hyperparameter optimization algorithms found that standalone LLM agents generally underperform compared to methods like CMA-ES and TPE. The findings suggest that LLMs are most effective as complements to, rather than replacements for, classical optimizers.
