## 🖥️ Hardware & History

### ECC and DDR5

ECC RAM uses Hamming codes to correct single-bit errors and detect double-bit errors, with DDR4 employing a 72-bit data width. DDR5 includes on-die ECC to address higher error rates from smaller, faster memory, but it does not replace system-level ECC, and potential interactions between the two remain a concern.

The original Apollo 11 guidance computer source code for the Command Module (Comanche055) and Lunar Module (Luminary099) has been digitized and made publicly available. ## 🌐 Networks & Proxies

### LG to ban residential proxies

LG Electronics will suspend smart TV apps that turn webOS devices into residential proxy nodes, following research that found over 42% of these apps contained such SDKs. The company is requiring developers to remove the proxy functionality or face app suspension, and it plans to strengthen app evaluation processes. LG also faces criticism for bundling McAfee software in its monitors.

- [LG to Ban Residential Proxies from Smart TV Apps](https://krebsonsecurity.com/2026/07/lg-to-ban-residential-proxies-from-smart-tv-apps/) — *krebsonsecurity.com*

### China advances national single-stack IPv6 network and its own surveillance-friendly protocol

China is advancing plans for a national single-stack IPv6 network and developing its own surveillance-friendly version of the protocol, IPv6+, which could make it easier to censor netizens or block traffic. Beijing is already exporting this technology.

- [China advances national single-stack IPv6 network and its own surveillance-friendly protocol](https://www.theregister.com/networks/2026/07/22/china-advances-plans-for-national-single-stack-ipv6-network-and-its-own-surveillance-friendly-version-of-the-protocol/5275984) — *theregister.com*
