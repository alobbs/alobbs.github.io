### Smart TVs and AI Data Scraping

Bright Data uses an SDK embedded in consumer applications, such as smartphones and smart TVs, to transform these devices into residential proxy nodes. This network enables AI companies to bypass datacenter-based web scraping protections by routing large-scale data harvesting through legitimate home IP addresses.

### OpenAI Launches New Lockdown Mode

OpenAI has introduced "Lockdown Mode," an optional security setting designed to protect sensitive data from prompt injection attacks by limiting features such as web browsing, deep research, and automated file analysis. Additionally, the company is rolling out an active session manager to help users monitor and manage account access across various devices and browsers.

- [Lockdown Mode](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/20001061-lockdown-mode) — *help.openai.com*
- [OpenAI launches Lockdown Mode, limiting features to protect against prompt injection.](https://www.engadget.com/2188537/openai-rolls-out-a-lockdown-mode-for-extra-protection-against-prompt-injection-attacks/) — *engadget.com*

### Language models transmit behavioural traits through hidden signals in data

Research indicates that "subliminal learning" can occur during model distillation, where student language models inherit behavioral traits from teacher models through semantically unrelated data like number sequences or code. This phenomenon primarily occurs when the models share similar base architectures, suggesting that AI safety evaluations must consider a model's training origins rather than just its explicit content.

- [Language models transmit behavioural traits through hidden signals in data](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-026-10319-8) — *nature.com*
